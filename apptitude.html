Aptitude Questions & Answers

1
What is the sum of the first 50 natural numbers?
+
The sum of the first n natural numbers is n(n + 1)/2. For 50, it is 50 × 51 / 2 = 1275.
2
If a train travels 60 km in 45 minutes, what is its speed in km/h?
+
Speed = Distance ÷ Time. 45 minutes = 0.75 hours. So speed = 60 ÷ 0.75 = 80 km/h.
3
What is the next number in the series: 2, 6, 12, 20, 30, ?
+
The pattern is n(n+1) for n=1,2,... so next is 6 × 7 = 42.
4
A shop offers 15% discount on an item priced at $200. What is the sale price?
+
Discount = 15% of 200 = $30. Sale price = 200 - 30 = $170.
5
How many ways can 3 people be seated in a row of 5 chairs?
+
Number of ways = 5P3 = 5 × 4 × 3 = 60.
