Previous Year Group Discussion Questions & Answers

What is the impact of social media on society?
Social media has revolutionized communication, enabling instant connection worldwide. It fosters awareness and community but also leads to misinformation, reduced face-to-face interaction, and privacy concerns.
Discuss the advantages and disadvantages of remote work.
Remote work offers flexibility, reduces commute time, and can increase productivity. However, it may cause isolation, blur work-life boundaries, and lead to communication challenges among teams.
How responsible are individuals in combating climate change?
While governments and corporations have large-scale influence, individual actions like reducing waste, conserving energy, and supporting sustainable policies are crucial to collective climate efforts.
Will artificial intelligence create jobs or lead to unemployment?
AI will automate routine tasks, potentially displacing some jobs, but it will also create new roles requiring advanced skills. The net effect depends on education and adaptation to technological changes.
Should the education system be reformed to meet future challenges?
Yes, education should emphasize critical thinking, creativity, and digital skills to prepare students for evolving industries and global challenges rather than focusing solely on rote learning.
