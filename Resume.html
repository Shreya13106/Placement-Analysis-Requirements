Resume Preparation Guide

Everything you need to create a professional and eye-catching resume

What is a Resume?

  • A resume summarizes your education, skills, and work experience.
  • It serves as your personal marketing tool to impress employers.
  • Common formats include chronological, functional, and combination styles.
  • Always tailor your resume for each job or internship.

Tips for Building a Resume

  • Start with a clear and professional header.
  • Write a strong objective or summary.
  • List education, internships, and key achievements.
  • Use bullet points and action verbs.
  • Keep it concise and proofread carefully.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Including irrelevant information.
  • Using unprofessional email addresses.
  • Spelling or grammatical errors.
  • Overcrowded or sparse layout.
  • Failing to tailor your resume for each position.
