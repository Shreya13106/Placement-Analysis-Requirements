What is a Technical Interview?

  • Focuses on your job-related skills and problem-solving ability.
  • Includes coding, algorithms, system design, or technical questions.
  • May have live coding, whiteboard challenges, or take-home tests.
  • Evaluates your practical knowledge and technical thinking.

Common Technical Interview Questions

  • Write a function to reverse a linked list.
  • Explain object-oriented programming principles.
  • How would you optimize a sorting algorithm?
  • Describe a challenging technical project you completed.
  • Explain the differences between REST and SOAP APIs.

Tips to Succeed in Technical Interviews

  • Practice coding problems on platforms like LeetCode or HackerRank.
  • Understand data structures and algorithms thoroughly.
  • Explain your thought process clearly during the interview.
  • Ask clarifying questions before starting to code.
  • Practice mock interviews and review your mistakes.
